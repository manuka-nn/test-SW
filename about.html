Our Story

Building Spaces
People Love

Swedish Walls was founded on a simple belief — that where you work and stay shapes how you think, create, and connect.

A Space Built for the Way We Work Today

Swedish Walls started with a vision to create more than just an office — a place where ambition meets comfort. With offices nestled in Colombo 05 and a hostel conveniently located in Katunayaka, our spaces blend Scandinavian design sensibilities with the warmth of Sri Lankan hospitality.

We understand that professionals today need flexibility. Whether you're a growing startup, a remote worker, or a traveller settling in for a few weeks, Swedish Walls adapts to your needs — without the complexity.

Every detail, from the furniture to the amenities, has been chosen to help you do your best work and enjoy your best rest.
